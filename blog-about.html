Meet the Developer, Lorena Maite Ávila


Welcome and nice to meet you! I’m Lorena, a New York-based freelance brand designer, helping entrepreneurs and business owners explore an intentional personality. Maite [ my -teh ] embodies my passion and creativity; it’s not just the name of my brand, but a heartfelt expression of who I am, as it also happens to be my middle name.

I’ve always been inspired by creative projects, and while I’m not entirely sure where that initial spark came from, the art of creative thinking and the flow of innovation have been lifelong aspirations. My journey in education took me through various majors until I found the courage to study engineering science, where I discovered programming, particularly C++. This introduction opened my eyes to the world of coding, something I had dabbled in during my earlier internet days by customizing classmates' social pages, which parked my genuine interest in working together with clients, allowing me to streamline storytelling and identity in creativity for their brands.

Drawing from my Salvadoran heritage, I've learned to embrace cultural appreciation, diversity, and representation in design, believing that a client's background adds unique value to their brand.I strive for my clients to feel satisfied and inspired, give them a reason to be proud of their own work and their journey that brought them to where they are now.

In my free time, I recharge through slow movements like walking, yoga, and pilates. I enjoy a good read, especially the works of Joan Didion, and others alike. I lovvveee exploring new places and in-person experiences, capturing the moments that inspire me along the way.
